2024

Transforming Transmissions

Prototyping nuanced data sharing models for generative media

Description

A generative sonic sculpture that tackles pressing themes of ownership, copyright and consent. This project explores AI and communication through the dynamic evolution of sounds as they journey from transmission to reception. How is the message distorted through this system, how does this reshape our understanding of ownership and creativity?

The 2024 Berlin installation of this project invited participants to share a story, define sharing characteristics, and join their modified story to a looping audio. The installation was complemented by a hands on workshop investigating both loss and opportunity via collective participation in generative systems.

Artists
Carey Crooks, Chris Hebert, Christine Meinders, Keri Paniagua
Location
Berlin, Germany.
Kurt-Kurt Project Space
Stack
P5.JS, GPT4o, Python
Role
Collaborated on design of technical architecture, material and form; Co-designed and led workshop

Exhibition and workshop taking concepts of generative AI authorship and consent off the screen and into the material and sonic realms for Berlin-based artists.
