POIETO PROJECTS

Viewers are invited to release a feeling of their pandemic experience over the past two years through an audio visual installation. Viewers manipulate a tactile controller with three sliders corresponding to anger, anxiety, and gratefulness. The sliders produce a sound and visualization pairing that the viewer can choose to donate to the project.
