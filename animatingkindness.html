Case Study

Animating Kindness

Prototyping Philanthropic Model Shaping

Description

Animating Kindness is an international collaboration between The Kindness Foundation and POIETO, to bring to life a collective understanding of Kindness. The project kicked off at an event on December 13th, 2024 at the Bendix Building in DTLA. Grappling with both the ramifications of current AI data practices alongside the novel capabilities of LLMs for collective participation, this project explored pathways to philanthropic use of donated data.
